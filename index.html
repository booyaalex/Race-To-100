Race to 100 Signups

Ladies and gentlemen, start your engines! Welcome to the Blooket Race to 100 event!


Over 11 rounds, players will race against each other and try to stay one step ahead of their opponents to cross the finish line in this high stakes competition! Additionally, stump your opponents and gain the edge, because all sets in this event are created by partipants!


So what are you waiting for? Rev up, hone your skills and Sign Up now! Games begin in June and run through July. You must create a question set of at least 10 questions. The most creative and interesting sets will be placed in the event. DM @Legend Plays for more information.
